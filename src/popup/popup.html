Kick Cleaner 1.0.0

This extension runs on kick.com and cleans the page for you.

What it does:

  • Removes the sidebar (#sidebar-wrapper)
  • Removes the channel chatroom (#channel-chatroom)
  • Removes the injected channel player (#injected-channel-player)
  • Removes the site favicon
  • Applies a blur effect to all images

Elements are monitored in real time and removed as soon as they appear.

Click the extension icon anytime to view this info.
